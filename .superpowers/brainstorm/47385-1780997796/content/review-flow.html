How do you want to review generated images?

After the AI generates nursery prints, how do you want to pick what goes to your Etsy shop?

A

Web Gallery (inside Open Generative AI)

A dedicated "Etsy Pipeline" tab inside the app shows generated images. You click ✅ or ❌ on each, then hit "Approve" to mark them for Etsy.

B

Folder + Notification

Images are saved to a folder on your Mac. You get a daily notification with a link to review them at your own pace.

C

Batch of Variations → You Pick

The tool generates 5–10 variations per idea. You pick your favorites from a grid, rejects are discarded, winners go to your approved list.

D

Auto-Pick + Quick Confirm

The tool picks the best image automatically. You just confirm or veto the single choice — minimal friction.
